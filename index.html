Arma 3 Mute

This project is for muting Arma 3 via the Volume Mixer

Download the Latest Release here

Be aware that the software is not signed at the moment and as such you will have to unblock the launch software before installing.

Right click the Arma3.Launcher.msi file, click properties and then at the bottom of the first tab you can unblock the launcher to install. I will be signing it as soon as I get some money together for it.
